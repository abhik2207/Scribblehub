About Us

Welcome to ScribbleHub, a platform for writers, readers, and everyone in between! Our mission is to provide a creative and supportive space for individuals to share their stories, ideas, and perspectives with the world. Whether you're a seasoned author or just starting out, ScribbleHub is the perfect place to hone your craft, connect with like-minded individuals, and explore new genres and styles. Our community is diverse and inclusive, and we value open dialogue and respectful communication above all else. So come on in, take a look around, and start sharing your unique voice with the world on ScribbleHub.

ScribbleHub is a free blogging platform that was launched in 2022 by Abhik Gupta. The platform quickly gained popularity among bloggers, offering a simple and user-friendly interface that allowed anyone to create a blog and share their thoughts and ideas with the world. In 2023, Google acquired ScribbleHub and incorporated it into its suite of online services. Over the years, the platform has undergone several updates and improvements, including the introduction of new templates, customization options, and social sharing features. Today, ScribbleHub remains a popular choice for bloggers looking to start a blog for free, as it offers a simple and straightforward way to publish content online. While other blogging platforms have emerged in recent years, ScribbleHub continues to be a trusted and reliable platform for bloggers of all skill levels.
